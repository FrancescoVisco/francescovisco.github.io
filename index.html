About Me

Born in Pistoia, Italy, in 1997. Graduated in mechanics, junior game designer and failed soccer player. Always passionate about music, film direction and video games, I try to combine these three passions in order to create projects that can convey precise sensations to the user. My games are based on a good polishing and a nice technical level.

Projects

COLORBOX

Puzzle platform game where the player has to combine colors to solve puzzles.

LEVEL DESIGN FPS UE4
Narrative fps in which you have to destroy the supercomputer A.D.A.M.
The project focuses on the level design of a specific level of an fps, in accordance with the needs and characteristics of the other levels.

LINE BREAKER

Endless runner where the player controls an object capable of moving vertically in order to travel
as far as possible and collect as many points as possible.

Analysis that aims to provide the tools necessary to build a strong horror narrative structure and define how this will generate a cycle of tension.

LOST IN THE UNKNOWN

First person exploration game where the player moves inside a dark place and through a scanner
he is able to perceive the structure of the place
where he is, in order to find a way out.

NEON ROLLER

NeonRoller is a 3D puzzle/action game where the player has to move a marble to reach the finish line and go to the next level.
