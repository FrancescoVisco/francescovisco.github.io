About Me

Born in Italy, in 1997. Graduated in mechanics, game designer and failed soccer player. Always passionate about music, manga, film direction, dreaming of conveying them in my video games.

User experience is at the core of my design philosophy. I believe that a game is not just a piece of software, it's an interactive journey that players embark upon. To ensure an engaging experience, I meticulously focus on user intuitive controls and player feedback mechanisms. Every button press, every animation, and every sound effect is crafted with the player in mind.

Last two years I have had opportunities to specialize in level design. The creation of a game environment is essential to guide the player and accompany them within the experience, each element placed speaks to the player and gives a number of possibilities and limitations.
