About Me

Born in Pistoia, Italy, in 1997. Graduated in mechanics, junior game designer and failed soccer player. Always passionate about music, film direction and video games, I try to combine these three passions in order to create projects that can convey precise sensations to the user. My games are based on a good polishing and a nice technical level.

Projects

COLORBOX


Puzzle platform game where the player has to combine colors to solve puzzles.

PROJECT ORB


Project Orb is a first-person puzzle game where a player has to guide an orb, capable of
activating elements, into a terminal, in order to pass the level room.

ELK CLONER


Elk Cloner is a 3D puzzle platformer where the
player controls a virus that has infiltrated
a computer to destroy its core.

NEON ROLLER


NeonRoller is a 3D puzzle/action game where
the player has to move a marble to reach
the finish line and go to the next level.

Μίασμα


Μίασμα is a casual game where a molecule that can store particles useful to purify the molecule contaminated.
The journey of purification of the molecule is comparable to the cognitive one of man useful to purify himself from the miasma.

LINE BREAKER


Endless runner where the player controls
an object capable of moving vertically in order
to travel as far as possible and collect
as many points as possible.

OTHERSIDE


Otherside is a puzzle game where two players
have to cooperate to link the two symbols
forming the Taijitu.

PAPER ABOUT HORROR SETTING


Analysis that aims to provide the tools
necessary to build a strong horror narrative
structure and define how this will generate
a cycle of tension.

LOST IN THE UNKNOWN


First person exploration game where the player moves inside a dark place and through a scanner
he is able to perceive the structure of the place
where he is, in order to find a way out.

BUILD IT!


Build it! is a medieval tower builder
where the player has to create a structure with irregular-shaped blocks
in order to reach the minimum height.

THE SUN IS OUT


The sun is out is a casual-adventure game where
the player guides a light on a journey through a dark world looking for information about it.

LEVEL DESIGN UE4


Narrative fps in which you have to destroy the supercomputer A.D.A.M.
The project focuses on the level design of
a specific level of an fps, in accordance with
the needs and characteristicsof the other levels.
