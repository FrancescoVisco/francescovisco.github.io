About Me

Born in Pistoia, Italy, in 1997. Graduated in mechanics, Junior game designer and failed soccer player. Always passionate about music, manga, film direction, dreaming of conveying them in my video games. My games are focused on everything related to the user experience, starting from the game feel, with specific attention to the control of the toy, up to the polishing, a set of important elements to give back to the user the emotions and the experience thought by the designer. Furthermore, my projects are characterised by a good implementation technique thanks also to my basic knowledge of C#, which allows me to prototype and make short games in total autonomy.
